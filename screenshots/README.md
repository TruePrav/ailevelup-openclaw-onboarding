# Screenshots

Browser screenshots of the onboarding form require a running dev server.

To capture screenshots:

1. Start the dev server:
   ```bash
   npm run dev
   ```
   The app will be available at http://localhost:4001

2. Open a browser and navigate through each section to screenshot:
   - Step 1 of 7 — The Basics
   - Step 2 of 7 — Your Life & Work
   - Step 3 of 7 — What You Want Your Agent To Do
   - Step 4 of 7 — Communication Style
   - Step 5 of 7 — Integrations & Access
   - Step 6 of 7 — Privacy & Boundaries
   - Step 7 of 7 — Personality Snapshot
   - Confirmation Screen

Screenshots can be saved here as PNG files named `step-1.png`, `step-2.png`, etc.
